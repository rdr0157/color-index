Hassler-McDonald 3-Lot BLA is a proposal to reconfigure the boundaries of three legal lots identified as Assessor’s Parcel Numbers (APNs) 350602420049, 350602410069, and 350602420045. The adjustment would consolidate the three existing lots, identified on the survey as Lots A through C, into two resulting lots, identified as Lots 1 and 2. The adjustment would better align the properties with existing agricultural and fence boundaries and is located generally east of McDonald Road, between Detour and Frog Hollow Roads, within the Agriculture Residential 10 (AR-10) zoning district. The application BLA26-05 was submitted on March 12, 2026. The application was determined to be complete for processing on March 23, 2026. The BLA was sent to consulting agencies for comments on March 23, 2026 The purpose of this BLA is to reconfigure the boundaries of three legal lots identified as Assessor’s Parcel Numbers (APNs) 350602420049, 350602410069, and 350602420045. The adjustment would consolidate the three existing lots, identified on the survey as Lots A through C, into two resulting lots, identified as Lots 1 and 2. The adjustment would better align the properties with established agricultural and fence boundaries and is located generally east of McDonald Road, between Detour and Frog Hollow Roads, within the Agriculture Residential 10 (AR-10) zoning district. Parcel A encompasses the majority of the northeast quarter and a portion of the northwest quarter of Section 3, Township 6 North, Range 34 East, as well as a portion of the south half of Section 34, Township 7 North, Range 34 East. It has an approximate area of 234 acres, excluding those portions lying within the County right-of-way (AFN 2009-07286), as well as any portion within the Lowden Irrigation District No. 2 canal right-of-way (AFN 409663). Parcel B encompasses the majority of the northwest quarter and a portion of the southwest quarter of Section 3, Township 6 North, Range 34 East. It has an approximate area of 128 acres, excluding those portions lying within the County right-of-way (AFN 2009-07286). Parcel C encompasses approximately half of the southwest quarter and a portion of the northwest quarter of Section 3, Township 6 North, Range 34 East. It has an approximate area of 78 acres, excluding those portions lying within the County right-of-way (AFN 2009-07286), as well as any portion within the Walla Walla Irrigation Company canal right-of-way (AFNs 30113 and 39182). After adjustment, Parcel 1 will measure approximately 234 acres and will encompass the areas described above for Parcel A. This includes the majority of the northeast quarter and a portion of the northwest quarter of Section 3, Township 6 North, Range 34 East, as well as a portion of the south half of Section 34, Township 7 North, Range 34 East. After adjustment, Parcel 1 will have a width of approximately 1,228 feet at its narrowest portion, located in the southeast quarter of Section 34, Township 7 North, Range 34 East. After adjustment, Parcel 2 will have an approximate area of 206 acres, encompassing the areas described above for Parcels B and C through the removal of the interior lot line along the northwest and southwest portions of Section 3, Township 6 North, Range 34 East. The resulting lot encompasses the majority of the northwest quarter and portions of the northeast and southwest quarters of Section 3, Township 6 North, Range 34 East. After adjustment, Parcel 2 will have a width of approximately 1,008 feet at its narrowest portion, located along the northern boundary of the northwest quarter of Section 3, Township 6 North, Range 34 East. The subject properties are located within the AR-10 zoning district, which requires a minimum lot width of 330 feet and a minimum lot area of 10 acres. As described in the findings above, both parcels meet the dimensional standards set forth in WWCC 17.18.
